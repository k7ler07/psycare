Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend these training and workshops? +

Our programmes are available for different groups, including employees, parents, community members, students and educators. We tailor each program to meet the specific needs of the group.

How long do the training/seminar and workshop sessions last? +

Depending on the type of programme. It can range from a few hours to a full day, while more in-depth training sessions may span several days.

How can I register for the programme? +

You can register by contacting us via phone (011-36044552), email (thepsycare@gmail.com), or through our website. Our team will provide details and assist with the registration process.

How can I contact you? +

Feel free to contact us at thepsycare@gmail.com, and we will respond to your inquiry within 1-3 business days.
