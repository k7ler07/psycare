COUNSELLING & PSYCHOTHERAPY

Counselling and psychotherapy help individuals of all ages manage emotional and psychological challenges. For children, therapy supports emotional expression and coping with behavioural issues. Teenagers are guided through stress, self-esteem, and mental health concerns. Adults receive support for stress, anxiety, depression, and relationship difficulties, promoting emotional well-being and personal growth. These therapies provide a safe space to explore feelings and develop strategies for overcoming challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions?

We're here to help answer any questions you might have about our therapy services.

How do I know if counselling or psychotherapy is right for me? +

If you're struggling emotionally or mentally, therapy can help by providing support and strategies for coping.

How many sessions do I need to address my mental health concerns? +

Therapy duration depends on factors like the nature and severity of your issues, treatment goals, and lifestyle considerations. Sessions are typically weekly, but can be adjusted to your availability (e.g., every two weeks or once a month). Your therapist will tailor the approach based on your needs.

How long is each therapy session? +

The first session, which is an intake interview, typically lasts 90 minutes. Subsequent sessions usually last 75 minutes.

How much is the charge for the first session intake interview? +

The charge for the first session intake interview is RM250 for individuals and RM350 for couples/spouses, and RM380 for families.
