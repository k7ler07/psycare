Psychological Assessment

A psychological assessment is a comprehensive evaluation conducted by a trained psychologist to understand an individual's mental, emotional, behavioural, and cognitive functioning. It can be tailored for children, teens, and adults, with the approach adjusted to suit their age, developmental stage, and specific needs.

Our Assessment Services

Mental Health Assessments

We provide mental health assessments to evaluate a person's emotional and psychological well-being. These assessments are designed to identify conditions like anxiety, depression, and other mental health challenges. The goal is to make an accurate diagnosis and create a tailored treatment plan, which may include therapy or support strategies, to help improve their overall mental health and well-being.

Personality Assessments

We offer personality assessments to better understand a person's characteristic patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving. These assessments help identify key personality traits, such as how a person responds to stress, interacts with others, and makes decisions. The goal is to gain insights into individual behaviours and preferences, which can assist in personal development, improving relationships, and making informed career or life choices.

Learn More

Neurodevelopmental Assessments

We provide neurodevelopmental assessments to evaluate a person's cognitive, emotional, and social development, especially in children and teens. These assessments are designed to identify conditions such as Autism, ADHD, learning disabilities (dyslexia), and developmental delays. The goal is to understand the individual's strengths and challenges, allowing for tailored support and interventions to promote their growth, learning, and overall well-being.

Learn More

IQ Assessments

We offer IQ assessments for children, teenagers, and adults to evaluate cognitive abilities like reasoning and memory. For children and teens, these assessments help identify learning challenges, especially related to ADHD, and guide appropriate school placement. For adults, IQ assessments help understand how ADHD affects daily life, work, and relationships, leading to personalized strategies for managing symptoms and improving well-being.

Learn More

Career Assessments

We offer career assessments to help individuals identify their strengths, interests, and skills, guiding them toward suitable career paths. These assessments provide insights into personal preferences, abilities, and potential challenges, helping with career decision-making, job placement, and professional development. Whether for students exploring career options or adults seeking a career change, the goal is to align personal strengths with rewarding career opportunities.

Mental Health Screening

We offer mental health screening to help identify early signs of mental health concerns such as anxiety, depression, or stress. Through brief assessments or questionnaires, we quickly evaluate emotional well-being and behavioural patterns. The goal is to detect potential mental health issues early, allowing for timely support, treatment, or further evaluation. Our screenings are designed to promote overall well-being and help individuals get the care they need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions?

We're here to help answer any questions you might have about our assessment services.

Who needs a psychological assessment? +

Children, teenagers, and adults facing issues like anxiety, depression, learning difficulties, developmental delay, ADHD, trauma, or any mental health concerns.

How long does a psychological assessment take? +

It varies depending on the individual's needs and the type of assessment. Typically, it can take a few hours and may require follow-up sessions.

How do I prepare for a psychological assessment? +

No special preparation required. It's helpful to be open and honest during the assessment. You may be asked to bring any relevant medical or educational records, especially if it's for a specific condition or diagnosis.

How can I contact to set up an appointment? +

You can schedule an appointment by texting or contacting us at 011-36044552. Our team will respond promptly to confirm your appointment.
